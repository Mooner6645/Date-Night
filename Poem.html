Love Story

I believe we are one story away from loving people deeply.
Its takes a 2am conversation about a broken heart to feel yourself soften.
You hear about the time a person had nothing but their clenched teeth, how they fought to stay alive when it was easier to give up, and suddenly you wish to protect them.
You spend an evening sitting under a darkening sky, sharing a tub of ice cream and, almost out of nowhere, you think "damn , I love this person".

So tell me your story, how you regret hurting the people you love, how grief clouds some of your Sundays, how sunsets remind you of your mother.
Tell me your story.
Expect me to love you.
